A little Story About Us

Our History

Inspired by Filipino warmth and hospitality, Ayungon SHAJ Flowershop continues to celebrate the beauty of every moment, ensuring that flowers bring joy to every home and occasion.

Ayungon SHAJ Flowershop was born out of a love for the natural beauty of flowers and the vibrant traditions of Filipino culture. Nestled in the heart of Ayungon, Negros Oriental, the shop began as a small family business, offering fresh flowers for local celebrations, fiestas, and special occasions. As word spread about the shop’s dedication to quality and service, it expanded to an online store, making it easier for customers from nearby towns and even across the Philippines to send flowers to their loved ones.
